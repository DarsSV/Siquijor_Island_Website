Welcome To Siquijor

🌊 Known as the Mystical Island of the Philippines, Siquijor is a paradise where pristine beaches and vibrant marine life meet centuries-old traditions.

Whether you’re seeking adventure or relaxation, we have the perfect itinerary for you.

Discover Lazi Church and Convent

A UNESCO World Heritage candidate built in the late 1800s.

Swim and Swing at Cambugahay Falls

Famous for its turquoise pools and rope swings.

Snorkel at Tubod Marine Sanctuary

Home to vibrant coral reefs and diverse marine life, including sea turtles.

Relax at Paliton Beach

Known as the “Little Boracay of Siquijor” for its white sand and stunning sunsets.

Explore Cantabon Cave

A guided spelunking adventure through underground chambers and rock formations.

Visit the Century-Old Balete Tree

You can dip your feet in the fish spa pool while learning about local folklore.
